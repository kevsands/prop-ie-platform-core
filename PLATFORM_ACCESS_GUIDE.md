## 🔐 **PLATFORM ARCHITECTURE**

### **Technology Stack:**
- **Framework**: Next.js 15.3.3 (App Router)
- **Language**: TypeScript (1,234+ files)
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
- **Authentication**: NextAuth.js with role-based access
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **3D**: Three.js integration
- **Charts**: Recharts for analytics

### **Core Features:**
- ✅ **Buyer Dashboard**: 8-phase journey, HTB integration, KYC workflows
- ✅ **Developer Dashboard**: Project management for 3 flagship developments
- ✅ **Authentication**: Multi-role access (buyer, developer, agent, solicitor)
- ✅ **Real-time Features**: Live booking, countdown timers, progress tracking
- ✅ **Financial Integration**: HTB government scheme, mortgage calculators
- ✅ **Advanced Functionality**: 3D customization, document management
